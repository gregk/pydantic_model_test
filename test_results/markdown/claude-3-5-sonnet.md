# Finding the Speed of a Train

## Given Information
* Distance traveled = 120 kilometers
* Time taken = 2 hours

## Formula
* Speed = Distance รท Time
* Units will be in kilometers per hour (km/h)

## Solution
* Speed = 120 km รท 2 h
* Speed = 60 km/h

## Conclusion
* The train's speed is 60 kilometers per hour (60 km/h)
